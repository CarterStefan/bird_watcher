WHAT IS BIRDWATCHER?

Birdwatcher a one stop place for keen twitchers to keep track of the birds they have seen in UK. Using our database of British birds, birdwatchers can see information about a particular bird and where to spot them, and mark those that have been seen. Are you up for the challenge to spot them all? If so please log in above or if you haven't signed up yet Register Here

UK BIRDS

We have put together a list of all the birds that can be found around the British Isles. Browsing through the UK BIRDS section of the site, you are able to view all the birds and click on a bird to view more information about it. This will help you find where to spot them to help you complete the challenge.

YOUR SIGHTINGS

Using our report sighitng feature, users of BIRDWATCHER can keep track of all the birds they have seen, what date they were spotted on and the location of the sighitng. This will save you keeping notes on your phone or pieces of paper. Simply click the bird you have seen, add the date and location and the sighting will be added to your list. You will be able to see how many you have seen to keep you motivated for the challenege.
